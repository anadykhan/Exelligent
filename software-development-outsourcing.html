Software Development Outsourcing Services

EXELLIGENT Infotech provides a full spectrum of software development outsourcing services, helping businesses achieve their technological goals efficiently and cost-effectively.

Overview

Outsource your software development needs to our experienced team. We cover all aspects of the software lifecycle, from design and development to deployment and maintenance. Our services are tailored to help you leverage the latest technology while managing costs and resources effectively.

Custom Software Development

We create bespoke software solutions that address specific business challenges. Our process includes:

  • Requirement Analysis

    Understanding and documenting your business needs and technical requirements.

  • Design and Architecture

    Creating a robust design that aligns with your goals and integrates seamlessly with existing systems.

  • Development and Coding

    Building the software using best practices and the latest technologies.

  • Testing and Quality Assurance

    Rigorous testing to ensure the software is reliable, secure, and meets all specifications.

Mobile App Development

Our team develops intuitive and responsive mobile applications for Android and iOS platforms. Services include:

  • UI/UX Design

    Designing user-friendly interfaces that provide a seamless user experience.

  • Native and Cross-Platform Development

    Building apps that perform well on specific platforms or across multiple platforms.

  • Integration and Deployment

    Ensuring smooth integration with existing systems and deployment to app stores.

Web Development

We build dynamic and responsive websites that enhance user engagement and support your business objectives. Our services cover:

  • Front-End Development

    Creating interactive and visually appealing web interfaces.

  • Back-End Development

    Developing the server-side logic and databases that power your website.

  • E-Commerce Solutions

    Building robust e-commerce platforms that provide a seamless shopping experience.

Enterprise Software Solutions

We offer comprehensive software solutions that streamline business processes and improve operational efficiency. This includes:

  • ERP Systems

Maintenance and Support
